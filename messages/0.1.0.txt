Changes in 0.1.0
==================

Initial pre-release of the TamarinAssist plugin for Sublime Text 3.
This includes support for:

* Highlighting support in Tamarin for Keywords, Logic, Functions and your own
  Facts.
* Compatible with all your favourite Sublime Text Colour themes.
* Tab autocompletion
* Snippets to get theories written quickly.
* Prover integrated into Sublime.
